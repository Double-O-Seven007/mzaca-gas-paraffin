About Mzaca Gas and Paraffin

Safe, affordable gas and paraffin solutions for households and businesses across Bloemfontein.

Established 2023

Built on trust, one delivery at a time.

Mzaca Gas and Paraffin (Pty) Ltd was founded in Bloemfontein in 2023 to supply gas and paraffin to residential, commercial, and small-to-medium business customers across the Free State.

Our leadership believes that energy access should be dependable, straightforward, and built on trust. That means a hands-on approach to every order and transparent communication from the first call to the final delivery.

Today, we supply LPGSA safety-checked LPG cylinders and high-purity paraffin to homes, hotels, farms, factories, warehouses and construction sites — keeping stock available even during peak winter demand.

Our Mission

To provide safe, affordable, and dependable gas and paraffin solutions while delivering excellent customer service and operating with integrity.

Our Vision

To become a trusted gas and paraffin supplier in South Africa, known for reliability, safety, and positive impact in the communities we serve.
